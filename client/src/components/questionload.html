A

Newton

Joule

The correct answer is Newton (A). Force is measured in Newtons according to the International System of Units.

2. Which of the following is not a primary color of light?

Physics 102
A

Red

B

Green

C

Yellow

D

Blue

The primary colors of light are red, green, and blue (RGB). Yellow is a secondary color formed by mixing red and green light.
